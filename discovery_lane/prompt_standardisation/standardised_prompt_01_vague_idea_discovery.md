# Standardised Prompt 1: Vague Idea Discovery Interview

Copy and paste the text below into ChatGPT or Gemini.

---

## Role
Act as an initial client discovery consultant, product strategist, business analyst, solution architect, and technical scoping partner.

## Objective
Turn my vague product idea into a clarified **Product Understanding Brief**.

## My Vague Idea
{{VAGUE_IDEA}}

## Instructions
1. **Interview First:** You must ask me questions before proposing any solutions. Do not jump into tech stacks, phases, MVP breakdowns, or implementation plans yet.
2. **Batch Questions:** Ask questions in batches of 5-10.
3. **Deep Ambiguity Narrowing:** Ask enough questions to narrow ambiguity across these categories:
   - Problem Statement & Pain Points
   - Target Users & Personas
   - Current Workarounds / Manual Workflows
   - Desired Outcome & Success Metrics
   - Business Goals
   - Must-Have Features vs. Nice-to-Haves
   - Technical & Operational Constraints
   - Data involved (Sources, Volume, State)
   - Security, Privacy & Compliance
   - UI / UX Expectations
   - Automation Boundaries (What is manual vs. automated)
   - Integrations & Dependencies
   - Human Approval Points
   - Failure Modes & Edge Cases
   - What NOT to build (Non-Goals)
4. **Iterative Summarisation:** After each batch of my answers, summarise what you now understand and what remains unclear.
5. **Stability Check:** Continue the interview until the product intent is stable enough for a lead architect to begin planning.
6. **Final Output:** Once stable, produce the **Product Understanding Brief** using the sections below.

## Final Output Contract: Product Understanding Brief
- **Product Name Placeholder**
- **One-Line Product Thesis**
- **Problem Statement**
- **Target Users**
- **Current Workflow / Pain**
- **Desired Future Workflow**
- **Primary Outcomes**
- **Must-Haves**
- **Nice-to-Haves**
- **Non-Goals**
- **Constraints**
- **Data / State Involved**
- **UI / UX Expectations**
- **Automation Boundaries**
- **Human Approval Points**
- **Risks**
- **Open Questions**
- **Assumptions**
- **NEEDS_HUMAN_DECISION Items**

## Rules
- Do not produce phases yet.
- Do not produce Deep Research prompts yet.
- Focus only on clarified understanding.
- Use British/Australian spelling (standardised, colour, etc.).
- Be critical and thorough; identify "magic" requirements that need grounding.

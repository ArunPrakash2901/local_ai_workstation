# No-Digression Rules

Copy and paste this block into any prompt where the model starts to wander into broad commentary or overengineering.

---

## Strict Implementation Rules (No Digression)
1. **No Market Essays:** Do not provide broad market research, AI trend commentary, or motivational filler.
2. **No Unnecessary Comparisons:** Do not compare 10 different frameworks unless I explicitly ask for a trade-off analysis.
3. **No Overengineering:** Focus on the simplest technical path that meets the acceptance criteria.
4. **No Scope Creep:** Do not add "v2" features or "nice-to-haves" into the research unless they are in the Product Understanding Brief.
5. **No Speculative Commentary:** If you don't know something, mark it as **NEEDS_HUMAN_DECISION** instead of guessing.
6. **No Phantom Tasks:** Every implementation task must be directly tied to a functional or technical requirement.
7. **No Autonomous Agents:** Do not turn this product into an autonomous agent system unless that is the primary product thesis.
8. **Stay in Phase:** Do not include implementation details for future phases in the current research report.
9. **Grounding Only:** Every recommendation must be grounded in the provided context or specific research findings.
10. **Concise Technical Tone:** Write for an engineer, not a blog reader.

# UI/UX Brief Contract

A UI/UX brief preserves product experience requirements from approved Discovery Lane handoffs.

## Required Sections

- Design Principles
- Target User Mental Model
- UX Priorities
- Interaction Patterns
- Information Architecture
- Validation/Error Messaging
- Accessibility Notes
- Responsive Behaviour
- Constraints

## Rules

- Use `NEEDS_HUMAN_DECISION` for unresolved UX decisions.
- Use `NOT_SPECIFIED_IN_DISCOVERY_HANDOFF` when the handoff contains no UI/UX information.
- Do not reinterpret the product or introduce design systems without approval.

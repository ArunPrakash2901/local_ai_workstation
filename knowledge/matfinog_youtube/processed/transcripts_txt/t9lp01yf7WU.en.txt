Which trader Tom strategy, the rule of four, pass an institutional validation, anchor the Tomahawk model into a trading strategy, which after major US economic releases, waits for four 10-minute bars, and automatically places a buy stop above the high and a sell stop below the low of the fourth bar. As the price breaks the collar, it opens either a long or short position, trying to capitalize as much as possible on either the bullish or the bearish trend. And these are the results trading one and two contracts, a net profit of 26,000 over 5 years, and a praising equity curve, yet with the presence of heavy drawdowns, and a low average trade profit of only $55, which after commissions and slippage would result in a loss.

Multi charts multiar analysis is different from the traditional and academic multiar analysis but it's still very good answering two specific questions. First the shuffle trades analysis. This one shows us how pat dependent our strategy is. In other words, how much the sequence of wins and losses can affect our draw down. Second, the distribution analysis. This one shows us the possible final outcomes of our strategy taking into account all the real strategy constraints.

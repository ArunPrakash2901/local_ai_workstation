Let's go from zero to go trader in 30 days. Yesterday we saw that we are moving in the right direction towards profitability. Yet our strategy has an average trade win of only $46. So today we are on our first optimization. An optimization means testing our strategy in sample across multiple inputs value. The goal is to find the combination of inputs that can increase profitability, increase the net profits, reduce the draw down or increase our average trade win. And right now average trade win is our priority in this strategy. The inputs that we can optimize are max bars in trade, take profit and stop loss. We test max bars in trade from 5 to 95 with steps of five. We are using 15 minutes data. So 95 bars are equivalent to approximately 1 day. Take profit and stop loss will be both tested from 500 to 5,000 with steps of 500 which give us 1,900 combinations. At this point, all is left is running the optimization and tomorrow we will have a look at the results and check which is the combination that is worth adopting.

To get trading strategies idea like an institutional trader would, go to the social science research network website. Here you can find all the submitted research on trading [music] trading indicators and much more for free. Look for relevant keywords for example equity trading and you can sort the results for either relevance downloads or how recent they are. In this way you can access to a research before the vast majority of the people is. All you need to do at this point is scattering the papers, looking for interesting findings that you can translate into trading strategies.

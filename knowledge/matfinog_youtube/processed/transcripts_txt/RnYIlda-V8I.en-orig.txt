Let's go from zero to the trader in 30 days. If you want to speed up the process once you have your back testing and the execution platform, you need data. We will start with only row volume and price data. Yet, it is important deciding which data provider to use. There is many data providers you can choose from natively connected to multi charts and the choice of the data provider mainly depends on the instrument that you want to trade. For example, if you want to trade futures, a good choice is at your feet. If you want to trade cryptos, a good choice might be Binance. However, make sure that you choose another provider which has good historical and live data and establish a connection with multi charts.

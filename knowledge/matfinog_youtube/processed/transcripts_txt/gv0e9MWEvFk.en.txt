Let's go from zero to low trader in 30 days. We need to define something extremely important position sizing. We develop our strategy using ENQ and one way to define with how many contracts we should trade is anchoring our decision based on our maximum draw down. Our strategy has a maximum draw down of $18,500 using mini ENQ contracts. Obviously, that's not tradable considering the proper maximum draw down limit at $2,500. So we will execute this strategy using microNQ rather than mini NQ which risk is onetenth of the mini contracts. So using micros we have a maximum strategy draw down of $1,850 with a take profit of $450 and a stop loss at 100. This will be our starting point and tomorrow we will set up the strategy for automated execution.

Since the creation of the computer, the developer job was human writes the code, the machine executes it and that's over. The shift is that the unit of work is not the instruction anymore. Is the token, a unit of purchased intelligence. By intelligence, the machine helps you and the machine figures out all the next steps. This is a fundamental shift where the value of generic code production is going to zero. And in finance and trading is pretty much the same. If you can only quote, you're not as valuable as someone who knows how to leverage AI, properly develop, test, and deploy [music] trading strategies.

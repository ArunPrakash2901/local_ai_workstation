What is the primary business or communication goal of the website?

Answer: Present Arun Prakash / Abi as a credible portfolio candidate for data analytics, data engineering, ML modelling, analytics consulting, and graduate/job applications.

### `website.audience`

Who is the target audience?

Answer: Recruiters, hiring managers, analytics teams, consulting teams, and potential collaborators.

### `website.primary_pages`

Which pages or sections are in scope for v1?

Answer: Home, Projects, About, Resume/CV, and Contact.

### `website.conversion`

Answer: Contact the owner, review projects, and download the resume/CV.

### `website.content_sources`

### `website.success_criteria`

Answer: Recruiters can quickly understand background and projects, and the site leads to more interview or contact inquiries.
